About Us




Search and View and download Class notes, Ebooks specifically from thousands of colleges



Upload and share study materials and get alerts when your classmates upload latest notes



Reduce photocopying and benefit from digital sharing real time!



Developed by Students that faced similar challenges and were determined to find a solution.

Jrox is the brainchild of Sudhanva, who is a Computer Science Engineering student from Dayanada Sagar Academy of Technology and Management, Bangalore. He encountered several challenges in collecting variety of class notes and books from various institutions, seminars and wasted a lot of time and money in photocopying the materials.

He saw that their peer group has similar issues in terms of accessing the right course materials and notes. This led to the birth of Jrox platform. To provide a digital platform for class notes, Question papers, eBooks etc. from across the globe and provide it to the students. Jrox also firmly believes that more knowledge you share, the more it empowers you.

Our Mission is to provide a Free (forever), simple and easy tool for students and teachers to share the notes. Jrox is a startup, angel funded by Raghav Belavadi, a serial entrepreneur from Switzerland and being passionately pursued by the founder and aiming to innovate more by building better services and make a digital revolution in the education sector.
