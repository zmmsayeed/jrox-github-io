About


We’ve heard the usual song of how terrible our current education system is, how we don’t have access to the wide range of study materials or how sharing notes is tedious all the time when ideas and thoughts need to so importantly interact and so on.

  • There exists a lack of efficient sharing methods and the burden of paperwork when sharing has to be borne by the student.
  • Their knowledge horizon is limited to the college libraries and limited amount of books known.
  • Also, students willing to learn something new, face the problem of unavailability of required study materials under one roof.

These were the aspects of our system which drove us to starting Jrox. We, at Jrox, realize the need for such a platform, for the need to digitalize or perish. Jrox aims in digitalizing education. In an era where almost everything from banking to ordering food has been digitalized, we understand the need to establish an efficient way of studying; not only by learning something for an exam but learning how it works, how we can use it in our everyday lives and how we can make the world a better place using this knowledge. For this, we want to give students an access pass to almost every possible intellectual material there is.
